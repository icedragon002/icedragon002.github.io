# About Silas Tang | Axiom 042

Welcome to my digital workspace. I am a system engineer currently focusing on system architecture, middleware development, and the hardware-software interface. I am also the founder and lead of the **Axiom 042** studio.

## 🛠️ Engineering Focus & Technology Stack

My engineering practice is strictly centered around three primary constraints: **Security, High Performance, and Low Power Consumption**. I approach system design with strict contract engineering, selecting the appropriate technology stack based on specific architectural requirements:

* **Rust:** Applied for high-performance core services, memory-safe execution, and OS-level modules where resource control is critical.
* **Go:** Applied for handling high-concurrency environments, distributed systems, and cloud-native infrastructure.
* **TypeScript:** Applied for agile business logic, API gateways, and system UI rendering to connect complex backend services with frontend environments.
* *(Additionally utilizing Python for data engineering and foundational research.)*

## 🔬 Research Interests

My current work and academic research focus on the following problem domains:

* **System Internals & Kernel Architecture:** Operating system internals, memory management, and Hardware/Kernel Abstraction Layers (HAL/KAL). This includes the customization and optimization of RTOS and OpenHarmony distributions.
* **High-Performance Middleware & IPC:** Architecting efficient communication bridges between heterogeneous environments. The focus is on strict contract engineering, zero-copy data transfer, and robust Inter-Process Communication (e.g., UDS, Protobuf).
* **Distributed Systems & Data Infrastructure:** Building the foundational infrastructure required for high-availability and data-intensive applications, emphasizing system scalability, fault tolerance, and efficient resource allocation.
